The Challenge

Competing in CBD and natural wellness products

The Competitive Landscape

Dragonfly CBD
UK Market Leader
Dragonfly CBD holds the strongest organic position in the UK CBD market, backed by a broad product catalog and years of accumulated domain authority from health media coverage and editorial reviews. Their early-mover advantage and established link profile make them the benchmark competitor for any premium UK CBD brand targeting informational and commercial search terms.
Cannaray
UK CBD Editorial Brand
Cannaray has built a content-led editorial identity in the UK CBD space, earning features in national press and women's wellness publications that generate high-authority backlinks and drive brand-aware search traffic. Their storytelling approach attracts a recurring audience that amplifies organic performance beyond pure keyword targeting.
Holland & Barrett
Retail Wellness Authority UK
Holland & Barrett's dominant UK health retail presence means their CBD category pages rank for nearly every broad wellness and supplement query, intercepting buyers who haven't yet committed to a specific CBD brand. Their domain authority built across decades of health retail gives any product they stock an immediate organic ranking advantage.

Provacan's clearest keyword opportunity is in clinical credibility and research-backed CBD content—bioavailability, dosing guidance, and condition-specific efficacy queries—where retail aggregators lack depth and editorial CBD brands have not invested in the scientific rigour that premium buyers are increasingly searching for.

QCK came in with a content strategy built specifically around the terms consumers seeking natural alternatives for stress, sleep, and recovery were actually searching. The goal wasn't vanity rankings - it was owning the searches that precede purchase decisions.

The Work

What QCK Did

Content Strategy

Built a comprehensive keyword map covering the full CBD and natural wellness products research journey. Prioritized by search volume, buyer intent, and competitive gap - targeting terms like "cbd oil" where real purchase decisions happen.

Content Production

Created and published keyword-targeted articles and landing pages at scale. Covered both informational queries (hemp products) and transactional terms. Each piece optimized end-to-end with internal linking to product pages.

Technical SEO

Full technical audit and remediation. Fixed heading hierarchy, rewrote meta titles and descriptions, submitted updated sitemap to Google Search Console, resolved indexing gaps, and implemented systematic internal linking across core pages.

On-Page Optimization

Optimized product and category pages for both search ranking and conversion. Rewrote CTR-focused title tags, implemented FAQ schema on informational pages, and aligned copy with target keywords while preserving brand voice.
