The Challenge

Owning search in a category where giants dominate

Koia makes dairy-free, plant-based protein drinks - products that sit at the intersection of health, wellness, and convenience. Their buyers are health-conscious consumers who research obsessively before purchasing. Search is where those decisions get made.

The challenge: this category is dominated by brands with $200M+ in revenue, celebrity endorsements, and dedicated content teams. Ranking for terms like "dairy free protein shakes" or "protein soda" means outranking competitors who've been investing in SEO for years.

Despite a superior product and loyal customer base, Koia wasn't capturing buyers at the research stage. Someone searching "is sunflower lecithin bad for you" or "lactose free protein shakes" wasn't finding Koia first - and those informational searches are where purchase decisions start, long before anyone opens the app to order.

QCK mapped every high-intent search across Koia's category - from ingredient-level questions to direct product comparisons - and built a systematic content program targeting the terms Koia's actual buyers use. The results compounded month over month, building a durable organic presence that grows without paid spend.

The Work

What QCK Did

A systematic SEO program built around how Koia's actual buyers search - not generic nutrition content, but specific queries that lead directly to purchase.

Content Strategy

Built a comprehensive keyword map across dairy-free protein, plant-based nutrition, and ingredient-level searches. Targeted high-volume terms like "dairy free protein shakes" (7.1k/mo) and informational queries like "sunflower lecithin benefits" (5.7k/mo) where buyers research before buying.

Content Production

Created articles and landing pages targeting every stage of the buyer journey. Ingredient-level questions ("is sunflower lecithin bad for you", "sunflower lecithin benefits"), product comparisons ("best dairy free protein shakes"), and category terms ("lactose free protein shakes"). Each piece builds authority and routes to product pages.

Technical SEO

Full technical audit and remediation. Fixed heading hierarchy across product and blog pages, rewrote meta titles and descriptions, submitted updated sitemap to Google Search Console, resolved indexing gaps on key category and ingredient pages, and implemented systematic internal linking from editorial content to product collections.

On-Page Optimization

Optimized product and collection pages for both search and conversion. CTR-focused title tags designed around how buyers actually search, FAQ schema on ingredient and nutrition pages to capture featured snippets, and copy aligned with target keywords while preserving Koia's clean, health-forward brand voice.
