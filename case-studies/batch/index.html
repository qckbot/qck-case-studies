The Challenge

Rebuilding from a Google algorithm wipeout — and coming back stronger

BATCH is a premium wellness brand selling high-quality CBD products including gummies, oils, and topicals. The category is one of the most competitive in SEO — regulatorily constrained, heavily scrutinized by Google, and crowded with well-funded competitors.

Through 2023 and into 2024, QCK helped grow BATCH from 4,803 to 29,849 monthly organic visits — a strong trajectory. Then Google's algorithm updates in late 2024 hit the CBD/wellness category hard, crashing BATCH from 29k to just 1,004 visits/month by January 2025.

This is the part of SEO most agencies don't talk about. Algorithm hits happen. The question is what you do next. BATCH and QCK didn't retreat — they doubled down on building a more durable content foundation.

By March 2025, traffic had already recovered to 22,940. By June 2025, BATCH hit a new all-time high of 36,377 — well above the pre-crash peak. That's what recovery with intention looks like.

The Strategy

How QCK Rebuilt — Better Than Before

Algo recovery isn't about reversing what happened. It's about understanding why and rebuilding with stronger architecture.

Algo Impact Audit

Deep analysis of which pages lost rankings and why. Identified patterns in the content that Google's update targeted — thin content, E-E-A-T gaps, and over-reliance on certain content formats in the CBD space. Created a prioritized action plan to address root causes rather than symptoms.

E-E-A-T Content Overhaul

Rebuilt top-performing pages with stronger experience, expertise, authoritativeness, and trustworthiness signals. Enhanced author credentialing, added scientific citations, improved content depth on wellness topics like "CBD for sleep", "CBD dosage guide", and "CBD benefits". Created new authoritative content in gaps left by competitors who were also hit.

Technical & Architecture Rebuild

Used the recovery period to fix structural issues that predated the algorithm hit. Improved site architecture to better signal topical authority in the wellness/CBD category, resolved crawling inefficiencies, and strengthened internal linking to concentrate ranking signals on the most commercially valuable pages.

New Keyword Expansion

While recovering existing rankings, simultaneously expanded into new keyword clusters that competitors abandoned post-update — targeting terms like "cbd gummies for sleep" (74k/mo), "best cbd for anxiety" (12k/mo), and "cbd oil benefits" (90k/mo). The double-down strategy meant BATCH entered the next Google evaluation cycle with a significantly broader and higher-quality content footprint — resulting in a new all-time high rather than just a recovery.
