The Challenge

SEO in one of the hardest categories online

CBD is among the most SEO-constrained categories on the internet. Google applies elevated scrutiny to health-adjacent products under its E-E-A-T guidelines. Regulatory ambiguity limits certain content approaches. And the market is crowded with well-funded competitors from Charlotte's Web to Medterra.

When Soul CBD came to QCK in June 2023, they were generating around 1,444 organic visits per month. A solid start — but representing a tiny fraction of the available search demand for CBD wellness products.

The opportunity was real: Soul CBD had a strong brand story, clean product line, and genuine credibility in the wellness space. What they needed was a systematic SEO strategy built specifically for the constraints of their category.

People searching "CBD for sleep", "full spectrum CBD oil", and "CBD benefits anxiety" weren't finding Soul CBD — they were finding review aggregators and competitor brands. That was the gap QCK closed.

The Work

What QCK Did

CBD SEO requires a specialized playbook. General tactics don't work here — everything had to be built around the category's unique constraints.

CBD-Safe Keyword Strategy

Mapped the full CBD search landscape and identified the keyword clusters actually rankable for CBD brands — wellness-adjacent terms, ingredient-focused queries, benefit-driven searches. Built a content map around terms where organic visibility is achievable: "CBD for sleep", "full spectrum vs broad spectrum", "CBD dosage guide". Prioritized by intent, search volume, and competitive gap.

E-E-A-T Authority Content

Created comprehensive, citation-backed content that meets Google's elevated standards for health-adjacent topics. Every piece positioned Soul CBD as a genuine authority — not just a product seller. Built topical authority clusters around sleep, stress, recovery, and daily wellness. Each article designed to rank for primary keywords while supporting surrounding pages through internal linking.

On-Page Optimization

Rewrote title tags and meta descriptions across product and collection pages to maximize click-through rates. Implemented FAQ schema on informational pages. Overhauled heading structures to align with target keywords while preserving brand voice. Strengthened internal linking to route research-phase traffic toward conversion pages.

Technical SEO Foundation

Resolved crawl issues and duplicate content from product variants. Improved Core Web Vitals scores. Ensured proper canonicalization across the domain. Fixed indexation gaps where pages existed but weren't being discovered. A strong technical foundation is what makes the content investment compound over time.
