4X ROI Guarantee
Terms & Conditions

Effective date: June 2025. These terms govern the QCK 4X ROI Guarantee available exclusively on the QCK DIY 12-Month SEO/AEO Plan.

1. Eligible Plan

This guarantee applies exclusively to clients enrolled in the QCK DIY 12-Month SEO/AEO Plan. All other QCK service tiers — including Managed Plans, consulting engagements, and short-term or month-to-month arrangements — are expressly excluded from this guarantee.

2. What the Guarantee Covers

QCK guarantees that, over the 12-month plan term, the client's total organic revenue attributable to SEO/AEO activity will exceed 4 times (4X) the total cost of the plan. If this threshold is not met, the client is entitled to a full refund of plan fees paid, subject to the conditions set forth in these terms.

3. Definition of "4X ROI"

For purposes of this guarantee, 4X ROI is defined as:

  1. Total organic revenue — the sum of all revenue attributed to the organic search channel (both branded and non-branded keywords) during the 12-month engagement period, as recorded in the client's Google Analytics 4 (or agreed-upon attribution platform) under the Organic Search traffic source.
  2. Exceeds 4X plan cost — total organic revenue during the term must be equal to or greater than four (4) times the total fees paid for the DIY 12-Month Plan.
  3. Attribution baseline — the measurement window begins on the client's confirmed onboarding completion date and runs for exactly 12 calendar months.

4. Attribution & Measurement

  • Revenue is measured using Google Analytics 4 (or another platform mutually agreed upon in writing at onboarding).
  • The Organic Search channel in GA4 is the sole attribution source. Paid, direct, referral, and other traffic sources are excluded from the calculation.
  • Both branded (e.g., searches including "QCK" or the client's brand name) and non-branded organic traffic revenue are included.
  • If a client does not have GA4 properly configured at the start of engagement, QCK will assist with setup. Revenue tracking does not begin until the attribution platform is confirmed operational.

5. Client Obligations

This guarantee is contingent on the client fulfilling all of the following obligations throughout the 12-month term:

  • Complete the QCK onboarding process within 30 days of plan activation, including providing all required access, credentials, and information.
  • Implement all technical SEO, content, and on-site recommendations delivered by QCK within 45 days of delivery, unless a written extension is agreed upon.
  • Maintain continuous access to the client's website, CMS, Google Search Console, and analytics platform for the duration of the engagement.
  • Avoid making major site changes (platform migrations, URL restructuring, removal of optimized pages) without prior written coordination with QCK.
  • Remain enrolled in the plan for the full 12-month term without cancellation.
Failure to meet these obligations voids the guarantee at QCK's reasonable discretion.

6. Exclusions

This guarantee does not apply and no refund will be issued if results are impacted by any of the following:

  • Algorithm updates — significant Google core algorithm updates, core web vitals changes, or other search engine policy changes outside QCK's control that materially reduce organic rankings across the client's industry.
  • Client site changes — the client pausing, restructuring, migrating, or materially changing the website without coordination with QCK.
  • Market or industry conditions — demand drops, seasonality, or competitive shifts outside QCK's control.
  • Third-party penalties — manual actions, spam penalties, or algorithmic demotions arising from prior SEO activity conducted before QCK's engagement.
  • Product/pricing changes — client decisions that materially reduce average order value, conversion rates, or product availability.
  • Tracking gaps — periods during which GA4 or the agreed attribution platform was not functioning correctly due to client-side issues.

7. What "You Don't Pay" Means

If the 4X ROI threshold is not met at the conclusion of the 12-month term, and the client has fulfilled all obligations in Section 5, the client is entitled to a full refund of total plan fees paid. Refunds are processed within 30 business days of a verified claim. Refunds are issued in the original payment method where possible.

8. How to File a Claim

To initiate a guarantee claim:

  1. Submit a written claim to support@qck.co within 30 days of the 12-month term end date.
  2. Include a GA4 export or screenshot showing organic revenue for the full engagement period.
  3. QCK will review the claim, including client obligation compliance, within 10 business days.
  4. If approved, the refund will be processed within 30 business days of approval.
  5. Claims submitted after the 30-day window will not be accepted.

9. Limitation of Liability

QCK's total liability under this guarantee is limited to a full refund of plan fees paid. QCK is not liable for indirect, consequential, or incidental damages. This guarantee does not constitute a warranty of specific traffic, ranking, or revenue levels beyond the 4X ROI threshold defined herein.

These terms are governed by the laws of the State of Tennessee. Any disputes arising under this guarantee shall be resolved through binding arbitration in Williamson County, Tennessee.

11. Modifications

QCK reserves the right to update these terms at any time. The version in effect at the time of a client's plan activation governs their guarantee. Clients will be notified of material changes via email.
